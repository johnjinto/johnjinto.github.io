Life is unlimited, but stay on this planet is limited

This app is not about life and death. It is about procastination.
More often people tent to procastinate things they want to do. This situation falls into the following categories

1. They are attracted to more fun things that give instant gratification
Thats OK.. beacuse, when a dead line nears, for example an exam date or a client delivery deadline, the fun is replaced with panic. The panic motivates to get the work done.
2. But in the absence of any deadline, the fun continues without intitiating the action that they really want to pursue. This can lead to regret and suilt feeling later in life.
